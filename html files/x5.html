A powerfully elegant character.

The striking twin kidney grille and defined LED headlights of the wide front end catch the eye. A character line rises from the rear doors, accentuating the vehicle’s athletic proportions. Powerful wheel arches and large alloy wheels enhance the off-road capabilities of the BMW X5 – and not just visually. At the rear, horizontal lines, sculpted bumpers, and integrated exhaust tailpipes underscore the width of the vehicle. All in all, a totally stylish and self-confident all-rounder.

Intuitive and customised.

The continuous BMW Curved Display combines high-resolution infotainment with intuitive operation, while the individually adjustable colours of the ambient lighting create a stylish atmosphere.

Quality you can feel.

The expanded Merino leather trim with decorative stitching lends a refined touch to the interior. It boasts a soft feel, elaborate stitching and premium seat comfort to transform every journey into a stylish experience.

Elegance and functionality.

Modern operating systems let you intuitively control the interior of your BMW X5 at all times. The prism effect of the lighting strip accentuates the impression of visual depth in the cockpit.

Technical Specifications

The BMW X5 is more than just a car; it's a statement of engineering excellence. By stripping away 240 pounds of weight and retuning the chassis, BMW has created a beast that tames the track and the street alike.

Feature Specifications
Engine 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (S68)
Power 523 hp
Torque 553 Nm
Transmission 8-speed Sport Automatic
Drivetrain AWD (xDrive)
0-100 km/h (0-60 mph) 4.2 seconds
Top Speed 209 km/h
Electric Range (PHEV) 62 miles
Fuel Tank Capacity 21.9 gallons
Weight 2.370 kg
Cargo Space 72.3 cu. ft.
