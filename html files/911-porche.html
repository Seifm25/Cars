Heritage and heart.

Sixty years in the making, this limited-edition sports car embodies everything that makes the 911 iconic: performance, precision, and timeless design. With exclusive design elements, unique interior and exterior color combinations, and Porsche Exclusive Manufaktur Leather upholstery included as standard for the first time in this generation, this edition offers the opportunity to own a powerful piece of Porsche history.

Curated color packages.

Five bold color schemes. Five iconic decades. Choose from five available Porsche Exclusive Manufaktur paint finishes representing an iconic generation of 911—Ruby Star, Signal Yellow, Riviera Blue, Brewster Green, and Fashion Grey—each styled with complementary leather interior and brake calipers.

Mark the milestone.

Limited-edition badging on the door sill, instrument cluster, and rear engine cover makes it clear that this sports car is a true rarity. The Sport Exhaust System and Active Rear Spoiler further underline its racing pedigree.

Indulge yourself on every drive.

This is performance luxury at its finest. Porsche Exclusive Manufaktur Leather upholstery comes standard exclusively on this model in three exquisite colors: Black, Bordeaux Red, and Crayon. Striking Carbon Fiber trim completes the cockpit, making the interior of this special edition sports car feel as legendary as it looks.

Technical Specifications

The Porsche 911 Carrera is more than just a car; it's a testament to decades of motorsport heritage and relentless engineering evolution. By mounting the engine behind the rear axle and fine-tuning every component for precision, Porsche has created a sports car that delivers unrivaled balance, exhilarating performance, and daily usability. It's a machine that feels just as alive on a winding mountain road as it does during your morning commute..

Feature Specifications
Engine 3.0L Flat-6 Twin-Turbo (Petrol)
Horsepower 388 - 394 HP
Torque 450 Nm
Transmission 8-speed PDK (Dual-Clutch Automatic)
Acceleration (0-100 km/h) Approximately 3.9 seconds (with Sport Chrono)
Drivetrain RWD (Rear-Wheel Drive)
Suspension Porsche Active Suspension Management (PASM) (Adaptive optional)
Infotainment System Porsche Communication Management (PCM) with 10.9-inch Curved Display
Performance Features Porsche Stability Management (PSM), Sport Chrono Package (optional), Porsche Torque Vectoring (PTV), Sports Exhaust (optional), Launch Control
Usage High performance & Daily driving
