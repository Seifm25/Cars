Technical Specifications

The Porsche Cayenne is more than just an SUV; it's a testament to decades of motorsport heritage and relentless engineering evolution. By combining a powerful twin-turbo V6 engine with the versatility of a spacious five-seater SUV and true off-road capability, Porsche has created a performance SUV that delivers unrivaled driving dynamics, exhilarating power, and daily family usability. It's a machine that feels just as at home on the highway as it does on a backroad or during the school run.
