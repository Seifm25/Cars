Astounding performance.

Born for high-speed dominance, the Audi RS6 Avant has achieved exceptional performance as one of the fastest high-performance wagons ever built. A 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers 591 horsepower, while the adaptive air suspension enhances precision and more dynamic handling.

Thoroughly track-focused.

Carbon ceramic brakes with red calipers augment the lightweight alloy wheels with RS-exclusive design. Conquer the road with ultra high-performance available tires, delivering exceptional grip and maximum control at every speed.

An air-slicing exterior.

The lightweight carbon fiber elements and aggressive front splitter enhance the exceptional aerodynamics and visuals of the RS6 Avant. A rear diffuser and roof spoiler improve downforce, stability, and maneuverability at increased speeds.

The seat of power.

Standard RS sport seats are lightweight and aggressively bolstered. The spacious rear seating and refined interior layout further enhance comfort while maintaining a performance-focused driving experience.

Technical Specifications

The Audi RS6 Avant is more than just a car; it's a statement of engineering excellence. By combining high performance with everyday usability and retuning the chassis, Audi has created a machine that dominates both the road and long-distance driving alike.
