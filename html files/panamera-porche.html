Technical Specifications

The Porsche Panamera is more than just a sedan; it's a testament to decades of motorsport heritage and relentless engineering evolution. By combining a powerful twin-turbo V6 engine with the practicality of four doors and a spacious hatchback, Porsche has created a sports sedan that delivers unrivaled grand touring comfort, exhilarating performance, and daily usability. It's a machine that feels just as at home on the Autobahn as it does during your school run or weekend getaway.

Feature Specifications.
Engine 2.9L V6 Twin-Turbo (Petrol).
Horsepower 353 hp (PS) – 260 kW.
Torque 500 Nm (369 lb-ft).
Transmission 8-speed PDK (Dual-Clutch Automatic).
Acceleration (0-100 km/h) (0-100 km/h) 5.1 seconds (with Sport Chrono Package) 5.3 seconds (without Sport Chrono).
Drivetrain RWD (Rear-Wheel Drive).
Suspension Porsche Active Suspension Management (PASM) with adaptive air suspension, double wishbone (front) / multi-link (rear).
Infotainment System. Porsche Communication Management (PCM) with 10.9-inch or 12.3-inch touchscreen, Apple CarPlay, navigation, wireless charging.
Performance Features Porsche Stability Management (PSM), Sport Chrono Package (optional), Launch Control, Porsche Traction Management (PTM), 14-way power seats, ParkAssist.
Usage High performance luxury & Daily grand touring.
