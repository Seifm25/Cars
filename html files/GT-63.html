Heritage and heart.

Seven years in the making, this limited-edition Mercedes embodies everything that makes the GT 63 iconic: performance, precision, and striking style. With exclusive design elements, unique interior and exterior color combinations, and Extended Nappa Leather upholstery included as standard for the first time, this edition offers the opportunity to own a powerful piece of Mercedes-AMG history.

Technical Specifications

The Mercedes-AMG GT 63 is more than just a four-door coupe; it’s a testament to AMG’s racing DNA and relentless pursuit of performance. By combining a handcrafted 4.0L V8 Biturbo engine with the fully variable AMG Performance 4MATIC+ all-wheel drive, Mercedes has created a machine that delivers unrivaled power and precise handling.

Feature Specifications
Engine Handcrafted AMG 4.0L V8 Biturbo
Horsepower 577-630 hp
Torque 800-900 Nm
Transmission AMG SPEEDSHIFT MCT 9 Speed Automatic (wet-clutch)
Acceleration (0-100 km/h) Approximately 2.8 to 3.2 seconds
Drivetrain AMG Performance 4MATIC+ (Fully Variable AWD)
Suspension AMG RIDE CONTROL+ (Air Suspension)
Infotainment System MBUX with AMG-specific displays and track pace
Performance Features AMG high-performance Braking System ,Rear-Axel Steering,Drift Mode
Usage high-performance circut capability & luxury Grand Touring
Weight 2,100kg
