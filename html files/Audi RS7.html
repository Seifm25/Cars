Astounding performance.

Born for high-speed dominance, the Audi RS7 blends breathtaking performance with refined luxury. Powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8, it unleashes up to 591 horsepower, delivering explosive acceleration and a top-tier driving experience. With its adaptive air suspension and precision-tuned handling, the RS7 offers unmatched control, making every drive as thrilling as it is effortless.

Thoroughly track-focused.

Engineered for ultimate stopping power, the Audi RS7 features available carbon ceramic brakes with striking red calipers, perfectly complementing its aggressive alloy wheel design. Dominate every curve with ultra high-performance tires, delivering exceptional grip, razor-sharp responsiveness, and confidence at every speed.

An air-slicing exterior.

The Audi RS7 showcases advanced aerodynamic design with lightweight carbon fiber elements, including an aggressive front splitter and sculpted body lines that enhance both performance and presence. At the rear, a sporty diffuser and integrated spoiler work together to increase downforce, ensuring superior stability, precise control, and confident handling at high speeds.

The seat of power.

Inside the Audi RS7, sport-focused seating delivers exceptional support with aggressive bolstering and premium materials. A driver-centric cockpit and lightweight design elements enhance performance, while a refined interior layout ensures the perfect balance between reduced weight and everyday luxury.

Technical Specifications

The Audi RS7 is more than just a performance car; it’s a bold expression of engineering precision and power. With a finely tuned chassis and intelligent weight optimization, Audi has crafted a machine that commands both the road and the track with confidence, delivering a perfect fusion of speed, control, and everyday usability.

Feature Specifications
Engine 3.0L M TwinPower Turbo, Inline 6-Cylinder
Horsepower 550 hp
Torque 650 Nm
Transmission 8-Speed M Steptronic Automatic
Drivetrain Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD)
Acceleration (0-100 km/h) 3.7 Seconds
Top Speed 307 km/h
Weight 1.625 kg
Seating Capacity 2 Seats
Braking System M Carbon Ceramic Brakes (Standard)
