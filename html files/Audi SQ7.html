Astounding performance.

Born for powerful versatility, the Audi SQ7 delivers exceptional SUV performance with refined luxury. A 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces 500 horsepower, while adaptive air suspension enhances precision, stability, and dynamic handling across all terrains.

Thoroughly controlled.

High-performance braking systems with bold calipers complement the robust alloy wheels with S-exclusive design. Conquer every road with confidence using advanced traction and high-performance tires that deliver grip and control at every speed.

An air-slicing exterior.

The bold exterior design of the SQ7 combines aerodynamic efficiency with SUV strength. Sculpted lines, an aggressive front fascia, and a sporty rear diffuser enhance both presence and performance, improving stability and control at higher speeds.

The seat of power.

Inside the Audi SQ7, premium sport seats are designed for comfort and support. A spacious three-row interior and refined cabin layout provide luxury for all passengers while maintaining a driver-focused performance experience.

Technical Specifications

The Audi SQ7 is more than just an SUV; it's a statement of engineering excellence. By combining powerful performance, advanced technology, and everyday practicality, Audi has created a vehicle that dominates both city roads and long journeys alike.

Feature Specifications
Engine 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8
Horsepower 500 hp
Torque 770 Nm
Transmission 8-Speed Automatic
Drivetrain Quattro All-Wheel Drive (AWD)
Acceleration (0-100 km/h) 4.1 Seconds
