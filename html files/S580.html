Technical Specifications

The Mercedes-Benz S580 is more than just a luxury sedan; it’s a testament to a legacy of elegance and a relentless pursuit of innovation. By combining a refined 4.0L V8 Biturbo engine with an advanced 48-volt mild hybrid system and 4MATIC all-wheel drive, Mercedes has created a masterpiece that delivers effortless power and unparalleled comfort.

Feature Specifications
Engine 4.0L V8 Biturbo with Mild Hybrid Drive
Horsepower 496 hp (+21 hp via Electric Boost)
Torque 700 Nm
Transmission 9G-TRONIC 9-Speed Automatic
Acceleration (0-100 km/h) Approximately 4.4 seconds
Drivetrain 4MATIC All-Wheel Drive
Suspension AIRMATIC® Air Suspension
Infotainment System MBUX with Navigation and Augmented Reality Head-Up Display.
Performance Features DIGITAL LIGHT, Active Multicontour Massage Seats, Rear-Axle Steering
Usage Supreme Executive Transport & Ultimate Luxury Travel
Weight 2,250 kg
