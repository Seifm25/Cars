Technical Specifications

The Mercedes-AMG E53 combines luxury, advanced technology, and strong performance in one stylish sedan. With its sporty design, turbocharged engine, and all-wheel-drive system, it delivers quick acceleration and smooth handling. Inside, the cabin offers premium materials, ambient lighting, and the modern MBUX infotainment system with dual digital displays. It is the perfect choice for drivers who want both comfort and excitement.

Feature Specifications
Engine 3.0L Flat-4 Turbo (Petrol)
Horsepower 429 HP
Torque 580 Nm
Transmission 9-speed Manual or 7-speed PDK
Acceleration (0-100 km/h) 5.1 seconds (Manual) / 4.5 seconds (PDK)
Drivetrain RWD (Rear-Wheel Drive)
Suspension AMG Ride Control Sport Suspension
Infotainment System (MBUX) Dual with 12.3-inch touchscreen
Performance Features PSM, Sport Chrono (optional), Porsche Torque Vectoring (PTV), Launch Control (with PDK)
Usage Used for daily driving, long trips, and high-speed performance with luxury and comfort.
